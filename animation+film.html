A moment up with the stars in

"SPACE ODDITY"

This animation based off of David Bowie’s classic 1969 hit came to life in 2019 for the final project in my Technical Film Production class, freshman year of high school. With limited understanding in animation, I chose to interpret the first half of Bowie’s song literally — illustrating the life of an astronaut delving into the vastness/beauty of space at the core of the story. After outlining the video with a storyboard, I created each frame — every line, patch of shading, highlight, or lettering — on illustrator with just my 2015 model MacBook air’s mousepad! As a novice animator, I resorted to taking screenshots of my work on Illustrator to mimic the motion/effect that an animation provides. As you can imagine, many screenshots were taken...

Software/Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Final Cut Pro, Apple Motion.
